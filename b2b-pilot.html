B2B Pilot (UK) — Product Intelligence

Non-commercial pilot to validate structured product evaluation in real business decisions.

Book a 10-min Pilot Call Request an LOI Template

What happens after you submit

  1. We review your submission and confirm fit.
  2. We request minimal product/context details (only what's needed).
  3. We deliver a structured evaluation report in the agreed format.
  4. We collect feedback to refine the framework for UK SMEs.

No obligation — and we do not publish or share your information.

How the pilot works (non-commercial)

  • Book a 10-min call
  • Share 1 product decision you are considering
  • We produce 1–2 structured evaluations (manual, template-based)
  • You receive a PDF-style report + short feedback questions

What you get in the pilot

  • 1–2 product evaluations using the fixed framework
  • Risks, constraints, suitability + recommendation
  • Optional seller/procurement angle depending on company needs
  • Delivered as a structured PDF-style report (static for now)

Who this is for

This pilot is designed for:

  • Procurement teams evaluating tools and products
  • Operations and warehouse managers making purchasing decisions
  • E-commerce sellers choosing products to sell
  • SMEs evaluating tools and solutions before purchase

What we measure

  • Decision time saved
  • Risk clarity (did it surface issues earlier?)
  • Willingness to pay (post-pilot)

About the LOI (Letter of Intent)

The LOI is a simple, non-binding document that expresses your intent to explore a paid subscription after the pilot if value is demonstrated.

What it means

By signing an LOI, you're indicating that if the pilot demonstrates clear value for your business, you're open to discussing a paid subscription. There's no obligation, and the pilot itself is completely non-commercial.

Compliance note: No confidential business data is required for this pilot. The MVP is a static demonstration; reports are produced manually.
