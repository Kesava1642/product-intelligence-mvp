1 What This Product Is

A semi-automatic carton sealing machine is a packaging device that applies adhesive tape to the top and bottom flaps of cardboard boxes. Unlike manual taping, the machine uses adjustable side rails and driven rollers to guide boxes through automatically, applying consistent tape pressure and positioning. The operator manually feeds boxes into the machine, but the sealing process itself is mechanised. These machines are commonly found in warehouses, fulfilment centres, and manufacturing facilities where consistent, fast box sealing is needed without the cost of fully automated lines.

3 Types, Variants & Options

Top-Only Sealer

Seals only the top flap. Lower cost, suitable when bottom is pre-sealed or taped manually.

Top & Bottom Sealer

Seals both flaps simultaneously. More efficient for high-throughput operations.

Uniform Size Model

Fixed rails for one box size. Faster but less flexible. Ideal for standardised packaging.

Adjustable Model

Side rails adjust for different box widths. Requires more setup time but handles variety.

Belt-Driven

Uses belts to move boxes through. Suitable for heavier cartons and continuous use.

Roller-Driven

Uses rollers for transport. Generally quieter and suited for lighter boxes.
